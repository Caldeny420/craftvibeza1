Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

1. Introduction

LexPilot Accelerate ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, interact with us, or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information (name, email address, phone number, WhatsApp number)
  • Business information (firm name, practice areas, location)
  • Communication data (messages, emails, WhatsApp conversations)
  • Usage data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent)
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party providers; we do not store card details)

3. How We Collect Information

We collect information when you:

  • Fill out forms or send messages via WhatsApp
  • Book consultations or purchase packages
  • Browse our website (cookies and analytics)
  • Interact with us via email or social media

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Communicate with you (including WhatsApp replies)
  • Process payments and fulfill orders
  • Comply with legal obligations (including POPIA)
  • Send occasional updates or service-related messages (you can opt out)

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service providers (payment processors, hosting, analytics – all POPIA-compliant)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator

To exercise these rights, contact us at the details below.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure.

8. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and functionality. You can manage preferences via your browser settings.

9. Contact Us

For privacy queries, contact us via WhatsApp at +27 76 431 2871 or reply to any message.

This policy may be updated. Changes will be posted here with the updated date.
