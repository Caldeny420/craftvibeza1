LexPilot Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 20 December 2025

  • 1. What Are Cookies?
  • Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, phone, tablet) when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences (e.g., login status, language) over time.
  • 2. Cookies We Use
  • LexPilot uses **only essential cookies** for core functionality. No marketing, tracking, or analytics cookies (e.g., no Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel). Our essential cookies include:
    • Session cookies: For login and navigation (e.g., remembering your authenticated state).
    • Preference cookies: To save your language or dark mode settings.
    • Security cookies: To prevent fraud and ensure secure access.
    These cookies are strictly necessary for the site to function and do not collect personal data beyond what is required for service delivery.
  • 3. No Third-Party Cookies
  • We do not use third-party cookies or trackers. All cookies are first-party and managed by LexPilot. No data is shared with advertising networks or analytics providers.
  • 4. Managing Cookies
  • You can manage cookies through your browser settings:
    • Block or delete cookies at any time.
    • Note: Blocking essential cookies may prevent access to certain features (e.g., login).
    For more details, visit your browser's help section (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari).
  • 5. Changes to This Policy
  • We may update this Cookie Policy. Changes will be posted here and notified via email/WhatsApp if material. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
