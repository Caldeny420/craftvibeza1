COMPLIANCE

Fully Compliant with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA)

  • 1. Our POPIA Commitment
  • LexPilot is 100% compliant with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA). We process personal information lawfully, minimally, and transparently to protect the privacy rights of our users, their staff, and their clients.
  • 2. Data Residency & Cross-Border
  • All personal data is stored and processed exclusively in South Africa (AWS Johannesburg region).
    No cross-border transfers occur without explicit, informed consent or where permitted under Section 72 of POPIA.
  • 3. Lawful Processing Bases
  • We rely on the following lawful bases under POPIA:
    • Contractual necessity – to deliver the LexPilot service
    • Legitimate interests – security, fraud prevention, platform improvements
    • Legal obligation – tax, regulatory reporting
    • Consent – marketing communications (revocable anytime)
  • 4. Accountability & Governance
  • LexPilot acts as both Responsible Party and Operator (Processor) where applicable.
    We maintain comprehensive records of processing activities (ROPA).
    Information Officer: privacy@lexpilot.co.za (registered with the Information Regulator).
  • 5. Security Safeguards
  • We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures:
    • End-to-end encryption (TLS 1.3 & AES-256 at rest)
    • Role-based access control (RBAC) and multi-factor authentication
    • Annual independent security audits and penetration testing
    • Regular vulnerability scanning
    • Mandatory breach notification to Regulator and data subjects within 72 hours
  • 6. Data Subject Rights
  • Data subjects enjoy full POPIA rights:
    • Access personal information free of charge
    • Rectify inaccurate data
    • Request deletion ("right to be forgotten") subject to legal obligations
    • Object to processing or direct marketing
    • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator
    Exercise rights via privacy@lexpilot.co.za or WhatsApp 076 431 2871.
  • 7. Sub-Processors & Operators
  • We engage only POPIA-compliant sub-processors bound by written agreements:
    • PayFast (payments – SA servers)
    • Google Cloud Firebase (auth/notifications – Johannesburg region only)
    • AWS (infrastructure – Johannesburg region)
    • SendGrid (transactional email – POPIA compliant routing)
    Full sub-processor list available on request.
  • 8. Staff Training & Internal Policies
  • All LexPilot team members complete annual POPIA training.
    We maintain strict internal policies on data handling, retention, minimisation, and secure deletion.
  • 9. Data Minimisation & Retention
  • We collect only necessary data and retain it no longer than required for the original purpose or legal obligations (e.g., 5 years for tax records, then securely deleted or anonymised).
