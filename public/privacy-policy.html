POLICY

100% POPIA Compliant – Data Stays in South Africa

  • 1. Our Commitment to Privacy
  • LexPilot is fully compliant with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). We are committed to protecting your personal information. All data is hosted exclusively in South Africa (AWS Johannesburg region). We never sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties without explicit consent.
  • 2. Data We Collect
  • We collect only the minimum necessary for our services:
    • Firm details (name, registration number, number of attorneys/staff)
    • Contact information (email, WhatsApp/phone number)
    • Usage data (billing, case management, invoices – anonymised where possible)
    • Payment details (processed securely via PayFast – we never store card numbers or sensitive financial info)
    • Technical data (IP address, browser type for security and analytics)
  • 3. How We Use Your Data
  • To provide and improve the platform: AI drafting, billing, CPD tracking, trust account alerts.
    To communicate: Onboarding, support, updates (via WhatsApp/email).
    Aggregated analytics: Internal improvements (no personal identification).
    Legal compliance: Retention for audits, tax, or regulatory purposes.
  • 4. Data Security & Storage
  • End-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest.
    Independent annual security audits.
    Strict access controls: Only authorised LexPilot team members under NDAs.
    Data stored solely on South African servers.
    Breach notification: We will notify you and the Information Regulator within 72 hours if a breach occurs.
  • 5. Your Rights Under POPIA
  • You have the right to:
    • Access your personal data free of charge
    • Correct or update inaccurate data
    • Request deletion (subject to legal retention obligations)
    • Object to processing
    • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator (South Africa)
    To exercise rights, contact us at privacy@lexpilot.co.za or WhatsApp 076 431 2871.
  • 6. Cookies & Tracking
  • We use only essential cookies for site functionality (no marketing or third-party tracking like Google Analytics).
    No cross-border data transfers without your consent.
  • 7. Changes to This Policy
  • We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified via email/WhatsApp and posted here. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
