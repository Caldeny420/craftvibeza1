POPIA
COMPLIANCE

Fully Compliant with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act

  • 1. Our POPIA Commitment
  • LexPilot is 100% compliant with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), Act 4 of 2013. We process personal information responsibly, lawfully, and transparently to protect the privacy of our users and their clients.
  • 2. Data Residency
  • All personal data is stored and processed exclusively in South Africa (Johannesburg region). No data is transferred outside South Africa without explicit consent.
  • 3. Lawful Basis for Processing
  • We process data primarily on the basis of:
    • Contractual necessity (to provide the Service).
    • Legitimate interests (service improvements, security).
    • Consent (where required, e.g., marketing).
  • 4. Accountability & Responsible Party
  • LexPilot acts as the Responsible Party (or Processor where applicable). We maintain records of processing activities and appoint an Information Officer (contact: privacy@lexpilot.co.za).
  • 5. Security Measures
  • We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures:
    • Encryption (in transit and at rest).
    • Access controls and RBAC.
    • Regular audits and vulnerability scans.
    • Breach notification to Regulator and affected parties within 72 hours.
  • 6. Data Subject Rights
  • You have the right to:
    • Access your personal information.
    • Request correction or deletion.
    • Object to processing.
    • Lodge complaints with the Information Regulator.
    Contact: privacy@lexpilot.co.za or WhatsApp 076 431 2871.
  • 7. Sub-Processors
  • We use trusted sub-processors (e.g., PayFast for payments, Firebase in SA region) bound by strict agreements. Full list available on request.
  • 8. Training & Policies
  • All team members undergo POPIA training. We maintain internal policies for data handling, retention, and deletion.
